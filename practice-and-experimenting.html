HTML Colors

Colors can be coded in three different formats;

By name: Red, Green, Blue

By hexidecimal: #FF0000, #00FF00, #0000FF

By RGB (red,green,blue): rgb(255,0,0), rgb(0,255,0), rgb(0,0,255)

By HSL (hue,saturation,lightness): hsl(0, 100%, 50%), hsl(120, 100%, 50%), hsl(240, 100%, 50%)

You can also use RGBA and HSLA, which incorporate an alpha value to control transparency: 100% Transparency, 75% Transparency, 50% Transparency, 25% Transparency
